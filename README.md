# Vijay Srinivasan, Ph.D.

**Senior Director & AI Center Lead at Samsung Research America**
*Large Language Models | AI Security | NLP*

I am an AI researcher and leader focused on advancing the state-of-the-art in **Large Language Models (LLMs)** and **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**. At Samsung Research America, I lead the AI Center in developing secure, robust, and instruction-capable AI systems.

### Research & Expertise
*   **LLM Instruction Following:** Evaluating and improving the ability of models to follow complex instructions through verbalizer manipulation and advanced evaluation metrics.
*   **AI Security:** Researching vulnerabilities in instruction-tuned LLMs, including backdoor attacks, prompt injections, and developing defensive frameworks (e.g., Poison,LLM).
*   **Mobile & Ubiquitous Computing:** Expertise in user modeling and mobile sensing from my academic background/Education.

### Education
*   **Ph.D. in Computer Science**, University of Virginia.
*   Active contributor and reviewer for major AI conferences (**NAACL**, **ACL**, etc.).

### Impact
My work bridges the gap between fundamental AI research and scalable, production-ready AI infrastructure for the Samsung ecosystem, ensuring next-generation devices are powered by intelligent and secure AI.
